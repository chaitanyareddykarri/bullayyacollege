Dr.L.Bullayya College of Engineering

Engineering College

Dr. Lankapalli Bullayya College of Engineering was established in 2010 by the Society for Collegiate Education to fulfill the needs of Technical Education in the North Coastal Districts of Andhra Pradesh. The College is affiliated to Andhra University and approved by AICTE, New Delhi.

Awards & Rewards

Dr Lankapalli Bullayya College of Engineering recieved recognition by NASSCOM National Association of Software and Service Companies (NASSCOM) awarded Dr Lankapalli Bullayya College of Engineering a Certificate of Recognition for its outstanding commitment to education and up skilling students in the field of Digital Engineering. Engineering College Principal Prof. D. Deepak Chowdary received the certificate at the Design and Engineering Summit 2023 held at Bangalore, on October 12th, 2023.




Dr Lankapalli Bullayya College of Engineering recieved the Skill Development Leadership Award

Dr Lankapalli Bullayya College of Engineering was awarded the “Skill Development Leadership” award presented by TIMES Ascent at WORLD HRD CONGRESS on February 15th, 2023 at Mumbai.
